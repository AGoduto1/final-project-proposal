Welcome to Lewis University's Customer Service Survey

Created by Angelo Goduto

Overview

The plan for this project is to create an HTML website and people will have the ability to describe their service at Lewis University. After they describe their answer to multiple questions that I will ask, they will have the ability to send their responses to my email at angeloagoduto@lewisu.edu.

Technology

I plan to use HTML for this project

Last Proposal Update

This proposal was last updated during sprint 6 on December 5th, 2021

Priority

My first goal is to create an interactive website where people will have the opportunity to answer the questions within the website. I believe this can be delivered.

Future Feature List

I plan to be able to take the information that is provided and have the ability to automatically send the information to my email. This may be a little more difficult, but I still think this is possible.

Basic Technical Features

The features that I will have provided will be creating a website that looks clean and has all of the questions that are easy to read for the person that will be interacting with the website. I will also be including some photos on the website that I create.

Advance Technical Features

The way I will make my project more advanced is by creating the ability for the participant to interact with the website. I will also give an opportunity for the participant to send me their survey after it has been completed.
